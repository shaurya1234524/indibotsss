Key Features of Indibots

Smart AI-Powered Chatbot

Indibots provides a customizable, intelligent chatbot for businesses. It understands queries, responds naturally, and can be trained with your own data. Whether for any business the bot adapts to your workflow.

Easy Customization & Branding

Add your own logo, colors, welcome messages, and FAQs. With a no-code interface, businesses can personalize the chatbot’s look and behavior. You can also train it with specific phrases, specific answers.

Real-time Analytics

Track user interactions, query topics, session durations, and satisfaction scores via an integrated dashboard. Get clear insights to improve communication and identify frequently asked questions.

Secure & GDPR-Compliant

Security and privacy are our top priorities. Your data is encrypted and never used for training.

Easy Integration

You get a simple JavaScript snippet to embed the chatbot into any website. Works with WordPress, Wix, custom HTML, or mobile apps. Just plug and play — no complex setup needed!

Chat History & CRM Export

Every chat is stored securely in the dashboard. Export conversations, gather leads, or forward summaries to your CRM system. Perfect for keeping track of follow-ups.
